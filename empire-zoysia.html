Empire Zoysia

Low maintenance, slow-growing and stunning dark emerald green – this is the Empire Zoysia.

Living up to its name, the Empire Zoysia turf variety is lush and luxuriant.  This quality turf is hard to go past for those who live in South East Queensland where the Empire Zoysia thrives, flourishing in our long hot summers.

Additionally, if you are looking for turf to place in shady areas, then the Empire Zoysia is the choice for you. Only requiring four to five hours of sunlight a day, this grass will not suffer from the lack of UV rays.

Thinking of using Empire Zoysia in Brisbane? City dwellers will discover Empire Zoysia is also suitable for urban or industrial areas with its above average traffic tolerance.

Those on the coast don’t despair; this all-rounder also grows well near coastal dunes.

Empire Zoysia turf is perfect for property owners looking for a low-maintenance lawn; the turf needs less fertiliser than all the others, in fact the Empire Zoysia turf does not need any in summer.

If your property meets the above requirements, then why not make it look like a true kingdom and invest in the quality Empire Zoysia turf.
