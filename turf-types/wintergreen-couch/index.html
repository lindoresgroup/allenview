Wintergreen Couch

Light Green, Fine Leaf, Great Value

A medium to low density lawn with a finer leaf than Greenlees Park. Tolerates filtered sun - winter green is an attractive low maintenance hardy lawn ideal for most domestic and sports applications.

Couch lawn obtains it highest quality finish when mowed with a cylinder mower, and can easily exist at a low height as long as it's high demand for sunlight is met, and it is receiving adequate water and lawn care. It is an unsuitable choice for shaded areas.
