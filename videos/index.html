Maintenance & Repair

Regular maintenance and repair is one of the significant aspects in making sure that your grass is in great quality. Apart from supplying premium-grade turfs, Allenview Turf extends its extensive experience and expertise in turf growing and production to its clients by providing a library of self-help videos.

We have a large collection of clips about lawn grub and disease control, preparing soil for new lawn, along with killing and keeping weeds out of your lawn. These videos can help allow your lawn grass to grow healthily, eliminate potential threats in the environment, and extend its shelf life.

We also have clips about watering and fertilising tips, which teach clients the proper way of providing care for their turf. Our lawn mowing tips show the dos and don'ts of cutting different varieties of turfs.

Expect us to share more helpful videos in the future. Our collection expands to keep pace with the advancement of technology in turf production. We will post new videos featuring the latest trends in the industry along with new techniques and devices used in lawn care. Contact us today for enquiries and more tips on lawn upkeep and repair.
