Turf Types

We stock the toughest turfs and lushest lawns in Australia

Wintergreen Couch

Light Green, Fine Leaf, Great Value. A medium to low density lawn with a fine leaf, Wintergarden Couch tolerates filtered sun and is an attractive low-maintenance lawn ideal for domestic and sports applications.

Sapphire Buffalo

Fine Texture, Durable, Deep Green. Sapphire Buffalo turf has a fine texture and a deep root system, making it the lawn-of-choice where you don't want a coarse leaf look. Its a tough grass that grows and recovers quickly and is renowned for its amazing deep green winter colour.

 MORE ON SAPPHIRE TURF →

Palmetto Buffalo

Broad Leaf, Low Maintenance, Dark Green. Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf is the most popular lawn grass in Australia. It's renowned for its deep and dark colour, superior quality and low maintenance. This grass grows broad hard-wearing leaf blades which involve less mowing and edging.

 MORE ON PALMETTO TURF →

Empire Zoysia

Low Maintenance, Great for Shaded and Coastal Areas. Empire Zoysia turf is perfect for low-light and shady areas. It's suitable for long, hot summers, and grows well near ocean and coastal dunes. This slow growing grass has great traffic tolerance and lower sunlight requirements.
