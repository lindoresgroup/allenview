Grass Types

  • ​Sapphire Buffalo

     Sapphire Buffalo turf has a fine texture and a deep root system. This is the lawn grass of choice of those who prefer not to have coarse-looking leaves. It also grows quickly into the ground, allowing it to recover fast from wear. This turf variety also grows an amazing deep green winter colour.    Read More →

  • ​Empire Zoysia

     Empire Zoysia is known for its dark emerald green leaf blade colour. The grass is low-maintenance because of its slow growth rate. It also has good traffic tolerance and only requires about 4 to 5 hours of sunlight, making it perfect for shady areas. This variety is suitable in regions with long hot summers. It also grows well near coastal dunes.    Read More →

  • Synthetic Turf

     Want something that's easy to take care of with little hassle? Then our great range of synthetic grass will be just right for you.    Read More →

  • Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo

     Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf is the most popular lawn grass in Australia. It's renowned for its excellent deep and dark colour, especially during winter. This Buffalo variety has a solid reputation for its superior quality and low maintenance properties. The grass grows broad hard-wearing leaf blades, which involve less mowing and edging.    Read More →
