Sapphire Turf

Back to Grass Types

Sapphire Buffalo turf is ideal for those who prefer not to have coarse-looking leaves. With its fine texture, this turf is sleek and smooth.

While it may be finer than other turf varieties, it is certainly not more delicate.  The Sapphire Buffalo grass is perfect for South East Queensland’s wild weather; the turf is hard wearing and grows quickly into the ground, allowing it to recover fast from wear.

Sapphire turf in Brisbane dwellings has become a popular phenomenon, partly due to its durability. Not only does it recover well after wild weather, but the turf also needs less water than other varieties (making it perfect during droughts), is both sun and shade tolerant and easy to grow. For South East Queensland residents, particularly those in Brisbane, it is hard to go past Sapphire Buffalo turf.

While attractive all year round, the Sapphire Buffalo shines in winter when it grows an astounding deep green. If you’re looking for a lawn that will be sure to grab attention and draw comments, while not suffering from the harsh and unpredictable conditions in South East Queensland, then invest in the stunning Sapphire Buffalo turf.
