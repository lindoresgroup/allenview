Grass Types

Allenview Turf is a great source of different grass types. With our wide selection of turf varieties, we’re confident to find the most suitable grass for your project. No two varieties are the same, as each type has a distinct set of qualities to stay beautiful under a particular environmental condition.

Check out some of the turf varieties we provide and learn the right type of grass you should use for your property:

Empire Zoysia Grass

Empire Zoysia is known for its dark emerald green leaf blade colour. The grass is low-maintenance because of its slow growth rate. It also has good traffic tolerance and only requires about 4 to 5 hours of sunlight, making it perfect for shady areas. This variety is suitable in regions with long hot summers. It also grows well near coastal dunes.

Palmetto Buffalo Grass

Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf is the most popular lawn grass in Australia. It’s renowned for its excellent deep and dark colour, especially during winter. This Buffalo variety has a solid reputation for its superior quality and low maintenance properties. The grass grows broad hard-wearing leaf blades, which involve less mowing and edging.

Sapphire Buffalo Grass

Sapphire Buffalo turf has a fine texture and a deep root system. This is the lawn grass of choice of those who prefer not to have coarse-looking leaves. It also grows quickly into the ground, allowing it to recover fast from wear. This turf variety also grows an amazing deep green winter colour.
