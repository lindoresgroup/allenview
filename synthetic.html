Synthetic Turf

Back to Grass Types

Don’t have time to mow the lawn? Too busy to pull out weeds? Don’t want to waste your weekends on your garden?

If so, synthetic grass could be for you. Lush and always green, synthetic grass is the easy way to keep your property looking stunning.

There is a range of options available with synthetic grass that you wouldn’t get planting ordinary turf. For example, novel ideas such as creating a chessboard or handball court with grass are catching on fast. Additionally, owners or carers of properties with synthetic grass no longer have to worry about fertilising, mowing, weeding or even watering their turf. The property looks stunning all year long, no matter the weather conditions. During the much-dreaded droughts that are not uncommon in South East Queensland, the properties with synthetic grass remain looking in optimum condition while their neighbours suffer from brown, dead turf.

Additionally, for those areas where grass just won’t grow, synthetic turf is the attractive alternative to cement or tiles. Synthetic grass will brighten up any area.

If you are sick of grass dying on your Brisbane property, synthetic turf is for you. Don’t spend any more tedious hours trying to maintain your yard, invest in synthetic turf today.
