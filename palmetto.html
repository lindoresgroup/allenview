Palmetto Turf

Back to Grass Types

Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf is the most popular variety of grass for Australian lawns. While Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf is renowned for its eye-catching colour that turns particularly deep and dark during winter, this is only one of the benefits of the turf.

The Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo variety has a solid reputation for its superior quality and low maintenance properties; for example, the grass grows broad hardwearing leaf blades, which results in less mowing and edging, and only needs fertilising once or twice a year. However, one feature is particularly popular – the ability, once established, to out compete weeds! Owners of palmetto turf in Brisbane find a lot less pesky weeds popping up.

Palmetto turf is also perfect for families, as the leaves do not irritate young skin like other varieties do, allowing for children and their parents alike to enjoy the great outdoors.

Additionally, if you’re looking for turf that will look after itself, then look no further than the Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf. Keeping its excellent appearance in full sunlight, yet still flourishing in the shade, this drought-resistant turf survives when many varieties would fail.

Perfect for both domestic and commercial land, Palmetto Soft Leaf Buffalo turf offers a range of other benefits, such as:

  • Disease and pest tolerance,
  • Good recovery from wear, and
  •  Low allergenic properties
